Ultimate Human System

Introducing the most advanced human systems ever created for Roblox! This comprehensive package includes a detailed suite of features that simulate human biology, health, mental states, movement, and survival elements.

Features:

  • Health System: Age, body type, fitness, stamina, organs, injuries, disease.
  • Stamina & Energy: Energy drains with activity, requiring strategic management.
  • Nutrition System: Balanced diet (carbs, proteins, fats) is essential for maintaining health.
  • Hydration System: Water is essential to prevent fatigue and health loss.
  • Sleep System: Regular sleep cycles needed to maintain peak performance.
  • Temperature System: Cold and hot environments affect the body (hypothermia, heatstroke).
  • Pain System: Injuries cause pain and reduce mobility.
  • Blood Loss System: Wounds lead to bleeding and blood pressure drops.
  • Reflex/Reaction System: Reaction time impacted by fatigue, stress, and mental state.
  • Mental Health System: Stress, fear, and anxiety influence body and decision-making.
  • Movement and Physical Systems: Realistic balance, climbing, swimming, and body damage systems.
  • Life Interaction Systems: Interact with objects, vehicles, and tools realistically.
  • Medical and Damage Systems: Handle injuries, infections, and diseases realistically.
  • Skill and Growth Systems: Improve through physical, mental, and educational development.
  • Combat and Threat Systems: Engage in realistic melee and weapon combat, with adrenaline and armor effects.
  • World and Environment Systems: Dynamic weather, seasons, disasters, and resource gathering.
